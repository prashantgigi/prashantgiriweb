PROJECT BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple numerical measure that uses a person's weight and height to gauge whether they have a healthy body weight. Here's a quick overview:

Formula:

For adults, BMI is calculated as: $$BMI = \frac{weight (kg)}{height (m)^2}$$

Categories:

Underweight: BMI less than 18.5

Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9

Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9

Obesity: BMI 30 and above
